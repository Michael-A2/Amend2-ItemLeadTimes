# Amend2 / Techna Clip — Master Sell Sheet Knowledge Base (REVISED — Full Verbatim Pass)

## About This Document

This document is reconstructed from a raw text-layer extraction ("richtexts") of a 164-page Canva design titled **"1 - Master List of Amend2 Sell Sheets."** The source is a compilation of individual product sell sheets for **Amend2** (magazine manufacturer — AR-15/AK/Glock/Sig magazines, magazine accessories, chassis systems, and promotional/UV-printed custom magazine programs) and **Techna Clip** (concealed-carry holster/accessory brand, owned/operated by Amend2).

This is a REDO pass over a previous extraction. The previous pass over-aggressively folded per-product feature bullets into a shared "deduplicated boilerplate" section and in doing so dropped or generalized away wording that was actually specific to individual products (different Mod generations reword Feed Lips/Material/Baseplate/Follower text; many SKUs have unique Grip, Baseplate, or Material sentences). This revision re-derives every product's full feature set directly from the raw source (all 4589 lines read sequentially, offset 0 to 4589, in ~30 chunks) and captures feature text VERBATIM (or extremely close to verbatim, correcting only obvious mid-word line-wrap artifacts) per product, even where it closely resembles another product's entry. Nothing has been collapsed into "see boilerplate" except the narrow categories described below.

### TRUE Boilerplate (verbatim-identical across virtually all pages, safe to note once)

- **Company/contact footer:** "Defend your right! Amend2, LLC, 4125 Bombardier Ave, Idaho Falls, ID 83402. See more at amend2mags.com" — appears on every page. Techna Clip pages replace the link with "amend2mags.com/concealed-carry/" and add the line "Techna Clip® is proudly owned and operated by Amend2®." FN SCAR co-branded pages add a second footer line: "71 Cogwheel Ln, Seymour, CT 06483. See more at kineticdg.com."
- **QR code callout:** "CLICK / SCAN QR CODE ABOVE FOR ITEM DETAILS" appended to nearly every Item Number/UPC/pricing block.
- **UV-printed magazine program links:** "To view all current Amend2 Custom UV-Printed Magazine designs, click HERE! Want to design a custom UV-printed magazine of your own? Talk to your local Amend2 sales rep to learn how. Click HERE to get started!" — appears on every custom UV-printed magazine SKU page.

Everything else — including Feed Lips, Material, Baseplate, Grip, Spring, Follower, Customizable Insert, Graphic, Limiter Assembly, Stripper Clip, Finger Grooves text — varies at least slightly by Mod generation and sometimes by individual SKU, and is captured in full below under each product. Where the wording is identical across a whole family of SKUs sharing the same Mod generation/caliber, it is written out per-product anyway per the task rules (duplication preferred over collapsing).
