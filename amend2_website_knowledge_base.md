## Company Background

Amend2® is headquartered at 4125 Bombardier Ave, Idaho Falls, ID 83402. Returns/warranty claims ship to a separate address: Amend2, Attn: Returns, 2184 Channing Way #129, Idaho Falls, ID 83404.

Amend2 started as a maker of budget-friendly AR-15 magazines and has since expanded into a broader shooting-accessories brand — magazines plus furniture (grips, stocks, rail accessories, trigger guards) and concealed-carry gear (Techna Clip, holsters, mag carriers). The company positions itself as serving everyone from casual range shooters to law enforcement and "elite special operators," emphasizing affordability without sacrificing quality, backed by in-house material-science R&D.

Techna Clip is a concealed-carry belt-clip/holster brand sold under the Amend2 umbrella (Techna Clip sell sheets also reference a co-brand address: 71 Cogwheel Ln, Seymour, CT 06483 / kineticdg.com).

### Magazine design lineage (per About Us)
Amend2's magazines have gone through iterative generations (Mod-1 → Mod-2 → Mod-3/Mod-A/Mod-C, etc.). Recurring engineering themes across generations:

- **Polymer body** — reinforced, impact-resistant polymer (fiberglass-reinforced nylon in most Mod lines; translucent polycarbonate in Mod-C) chosen for rigidity/durability without sacrificing function; textured finish with raised lettering for grip.
- **Follower/feed lips** — anti-tilt, self-lubricating "super follower" sitting at a higher angle than typical, paired with enhanced feed lips, for quicker cycling and fewer failures to feed.
- **Spring** — heat-treated, non-memory, non-corrosive stainless steel spring.
- **Baseplate** — sleek, low-profile design with a paint-pen matrix for marking, a patent-pending "2" baseplate, and tool-less disassembly button.
- **Customization** — the mold supports swapping three inserts (caliber insert, main logo insert, floor plate insert), enabling private-label runs. Private-label minimums start at 4,000 magazines; contact required for scope/pricing.
- **Warranty** — Amend2 advertises an industry-leading lifetime warranty on its products; warranty claims go through customer@amend2mags.com.

## Dealer / Wholesale Program

Amend2 runs a dealer portal (amend2mags.com/dealers) offering dealer pricing, priority fulfillment, and dealer-only products. Programs include:

- **Mag of the Month — Dealer Program**: monthly limited-edition magazines; minimum order 10 units, standard tier 25 units, volume tier 50 units; custom quotes above 50 via rep.
- **Custom Magazine Program — Standard Printing**: customer logo printed on Mod-A or Mod-3 platform; minimum order 50 units; no cap on order quantity.
- **Private Label**: full brand ownership (custom inserts, packaging, dedicated account management); minimums vary by project scope, contact required.
- Dealer resources referenced (routed through Contact Us): Line Sheet download, Brand Assets & Logos, MAP Policy, Dealer Application.
- Sales rep support: Mon–Fri, 9am–5pm MT.

## Policies (Customer-Facing)

**Shipping**: Flat $5.99 for orders under $75; free shipping over $75. Orders typically filled within 48 hours; shipped via USPS/FedEx/UPS Ground. No international shipping — billing and shipping must both be U.S. addresses (export-control considerations apply to firearm-related products).

**Sales tax**: ~6.0% collected on orders shipping to Idaho (subject to change); other states' use tax is the buyer's responsibility where applicable.

**Payment methods**: Visa, MasterCard, Discover, American Express.

**Returns**: Refunds only for purchases made directly on amend2mags.com, within 60 days of purchase. Customer is responsible for return/redelivery shipping. Wrong or damaged items are replaced or refunded (merchandise + shipping) at Amend2's discretion.

**Warranty / defective products**: Lifetime warranty; contact customer@amend2mags.com to start a claim. Returns/exchanges ship to the Attn: Returns address above (prepaid only, no COD); include name, address, phone, email, reason for return, and receipt if possible. Processing can take up to 10 business days.
